# 📊 AtliQ – Sales & Finance Analytics Report 

## 📌 Introduction
**Atliq Hardware** is a global manufacturer and distributor of PCs, peripherals, networking, and storage devices. Its products reach customers worldwide through retail partners like Croma, Best Buy, Flipkart, and Amazon. 

To support data-driven decision-making, I designed a Business Intelligence (BI) Dashboard in Excel for Sales & Financial Analytics. This project enables Atliq Hardware to track performance, profitability, and identify actionable growth opportunities.

## 🎯 Problem Statement
Develop an interactive Sales & Finance Analytics Dashboard that delivers insights into:
*   Revenue growth & customer performance
*   Market vs. Target performance
*   Division & product-level trends
*   Profitability across regions & time periods

---

## 🔑 Project Objectives

### Sales Analysis
*   **Track Growth:** Monitor Customer Net Sales and YoY revenue growth.
*   **Target Comparison:** Compare Market Performance against set targets.
*   **Product Performance:** Identify the Top 10 Products by Sales Growth (2020–2021) and highlight the Top 5 & Bottom 5 Products by Quantity Sold.
*   **New Launches:** Monitor New Product Launches (2021) and their contribution to overall revenue.
*   **Geographic Reach:** Discover the Top 5 Countries by Sales (2021).

### Financial Analysis
*   **P&L Reporting:** Build Yearly & Quarterly Profit & Loss (P&L) Reports.
*   **Regional Profitability:** Compare Market-Specific P&L to assess health across different geographies.
*   **Margin Tracking:** Track Quarterly Gross Margin % by Location for effective cost control.

*   **Microsoft Excel:** Core platform for data manipulation and visualization

*   **Power Pivot:** Data Modeling and building table relationships
*   **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions):** Creating custom KPIs and Calculated Measures
*   **Pivot Tables & Charts:** Visualization and Dashboard Design
*   **Core Concepts:** Sales Analytics, Financial Analytics, KPI Reporting, Data Storytelling

## 📊 Key Insights & Findings

*   🚀 **Revenue Growth:** Sales jumped from $87.5M in 2019 to $598.9M in 2021, representing a massive **304% growth**.
*   🏆 **Top Products:** Flagship items like the *AQ Electron 4 3600* drove major sales volume.
*   🌍 **Top Markets:** India led the market with **$161.3M** in sales, followed closely by the USA, South Korea, UK, and Canada.
*   🛒 **Division Analysis:** The PC division saw a **4x growth** from 2020 to 2021, while peripherals grew by **321%**.
*   📈 **Profitability:** Gross margin % remained stable despite rapid revenue expansion.
*   🌐 **Regional Trends:** Japan & New Zealand generated the highest profit margins, whereas Germany lagged behind.
*   🆕 **New Launches:** 16 new products were added to the portfolio in 2021, contributing significantly to the yearly sales bump.
